About

I’m Donovan West, a 20-year-old photographer from St. Louis and a sophomore at Central Methodist University. I play both basketball and soccer, and I’ve always had a passion for staying active and pushing myself to grow. Photography has been something I’ve loved for a long time. What started as just taking pictures for fun turned into something much bigger. I enjoy capturing real moments and creating images that people can look back on for years. Whether I’m working with individuals, brands, or creative projects, my goal is simple — to create timeless, high-quality photos that feel real and meaningful. I’m always learning, improving, and putting my heart into every shot.

My Approach

My work focuses on clean composition, natural emotion, and elevated editing. Whether we’re shooting portraits, lifestyle, or brand content, the goal is always the same: photographs that feel honest and cinematic.

  • Brand & product content
